HUD (Heads Up Display)

Overview

HUD is a repository for all listings that you bookmark in Marketplace. From HUD, you can quickly find, launch or get help relating to each listing. Also, you can remove the bookmark from a listing.


HUD

Accessing HUD

You can access HUD two ways using the Global Toolbar:
  • Click the icon on the upper-left side of the toolbar or click the  icon on the far-right side of the toolbar, then select Bookmarks:
