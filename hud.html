HUD (Heads Up Display)

Overview

HUD is a repository for all listings that you bookmark in Marketplace. From HUD, you can quickly find, launch or get help relating to each listing. Also, you can remove the bookmark from a listing.

Accessing HUD

You can access HUD two ways using the Global Toolbar:

  • Click the icon on the upper-left side of the toolbar or click the  icon on the far-right side of the toolbar, then select Bookmarks:
    Accessing HUD from the Global Toolbar

 

Listing Actions from HUD

From HUD you can open, get help or remove bookmarks for a listing. Each action is found under the drop-down listing menu:

Drop-down listing actions menu

This section describes lisitng commands--accessible from the drop-down arrow on the right side of the listing:

  • Launch in Webtop -- Opens the lisitng in your HUD.
  • Launch in new tab -- Opens the listing in a new browser tab.
  • Get help -- If the listing includes Help resources, they will open.
  • Remove Bookmark -- Deletes the listing from your HUD. To add it again, search for the listing in Marketplace.
